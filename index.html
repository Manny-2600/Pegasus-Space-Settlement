More About Peagasus

The Pegasus Space Settlement is an orginization focused on developing and supporting space colonization. We focus on four major departments and they are listed below.

  • - Food Production
  • - Transportation

Meet The Team

Food Production

Our job is to support and provide our community with healthy food to eat. We create healthy food that lets our settlers grow and prosper.

Transportation

Our job is to make forms of travel safe and efficient in our community so our citizens can travel and our residents have everyday access to anything they need.

Recreation

Our job is to create opportunities and encourage execise and fitness on these settlements, so that we can give settlers a healthy and sustainable lifestyle.

Residential

Our job is to create sturdy and reliable houses that help withstand any conditions with modern technology to create safe, and prosperous communities.

We are growing tomatoes and carrots, as well as cucumbers. We have cows and chicken, for meat and milk and eggs. We are using vertical farming and aquaculture to grow fish and algae, which can be used to make yeast and flour. We also have orchards for apples, mangoes, and oranges. We also have cacao plants and a chocolate processing facility to make chocolate. We have a processing plant to make energy bars. We have brought up canned/freeze dried supplies as well, to make sure there is extra in case of emergencies, like mad cow disease or something. We will not be bringing all of our food supplies at once, because the livestock should be treated humanely. We also have a bee colony as well as a big supermarket.

Public transportation could be a train that goes around the whole settlement, with different stops along the way. It would be easy and accessible to families, or just convenient because it will be running 24 hours a day. For private transportation we will make hoverboards that are electric powered that go 30 MPH Max and is very easy to handle and use. This is a good idea because it would not be using gas and make the settlement smell bad and smell like gas. We will make them go up to 20-30 feet in the air. We will also make small hoverboards that go up to 5-15 MPH and 3-15 Feet in the air. We will make the hoverboards that go slower and less high in the air ones for kids. They would be safe because we can make shoes with very strong magnets in the soles and magnets in the boards that make it so that you can't fall off. There is a button on the side of the board that has a cover on it and a button under the cover so you cannot get off unless you are trying to. There would also be obstacle avoidance programmed into these hoverboards.

We will bring a customizable piece of land, or more specifically, an area in the colonization that you can customize to whatever shape or size you want. One day of the week it can be a baseball field, then another it can be a forest, and then if your feeling like playing volleyball, you can even make it a volleyball court! We will also have more typical things, like a pool, a lounge/library, a movie theater, and small things like scooters and bikes. What we want for our space settlement to be different everyday, as a day on Earth would. There will also be a park for a place to relax and chill.

For the housing we will have rectangular apartments and six apartment buildings. The apartment buildings will be made out of smart bricks and insulated with frozen smoke, they will have bamboo panelling on the outside so that the apartments can conserve heat. The insides will include, a bathroom, a small bedroom, a kitchen, an office and a living room. Look at example B to see what the apartments should look like. For the transportation in and out of the apartments there will be elevators throughout the buildings.
